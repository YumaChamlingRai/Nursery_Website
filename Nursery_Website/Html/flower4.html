English Rose

Description

English Rose, also known as David Austin Roses, is a class of hybrid roses that are known for their old-fashioned, highly fragrant blooms and repeat-flowering habits. They were created by the English rose breeder David Austin in the 1960s by crossing old-fashioned roses with modern hybrid tea roses.English Roses are renowned for their exquisite beauty and rich, complex fragrance, which is often described as a combination of myrrh, vanilla, and fruit. They are available in a range of colors, from soft pastels to rich, vibrant hues, and their petals are often ruffled and arranged in a cup-like shape.

English Roses have become increasingly popular in recent years, both as garden plants and as cut flowers. They are often used in wedding bouquets and floral arrangements due to their romantic, timeless beauty and heady fragrance. Some popular varieties of English Roses include‘Graham Thomas’, ‘Mary Rose’, and ‘Jude the Obscure’.
